Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 3/4 cup cocoa
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup vegetable oil
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda
    and salt.  Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Mix until smooth.  Stir in
    boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
 
 2. Make donut shaped form. Deep fry until well-done.
